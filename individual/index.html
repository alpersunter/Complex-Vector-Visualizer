Complex number \(\color{rgb(79, 175, 214)}v:\)

Complex number \(\color{rgb(251, 139, 36)}w:\)

\(\color{rgb(251, 139, 36)}w\), is \(v\cdot\overline{w}\) much similar to \(\color{rgb(79, 175, 214)}v\):

Similarity between two complex numbers. (More "real positive" means more similar.)

How is it calculated?

Polar form

Assume \(\color{rgb(79, 175, 214)}v = ae^{i\color{rgb(59, 155, 214)}\alpha}\) and \(\color{rgb(251, 139, 36)}w = be^{i\color{rgb(251, 119, 16)}\beta}\). Therefore\(\overline{\color{rgb(251, 139, 36)}w}=be^{i(-\color{rgb(251, 119, 16)}\beta)}\).
\( \color{rgb(79, 175, 214)}v \cdot \overline{\color{rgb(251, 139, 36)}w} = ae^{i\color{rgb(59, 155, 214)}\alpha}\cdot be^{i(-\color{rgb(251, 119, 16)}\beta)}\)
\(= abe^{i(\color{rgb(59, 155, 214)}\alpha - \color{rgb(251, 119, 16)}\beta)}\)

Rectangular form

Assume \(\color{rgb(79, 175, 214)}v = a + b\color{#CD0000}i\) and \(\color{rgb(251, 139, 36)}w = c + d\color{#CD0000}i\). Therefore\(\overline{\color{rgb(251, 139, 36)}w}=c-d\color{#CD0000}i\).
\( \color{rgb(79, 175, 214)}v \cdot \overline{\color{rgb(251, 139, 36)}w} = (a+b\color{#CD0000}i)\cdot(c-d\color{#CD0000}i)\)
\(= ac -ad\color{#CD0000}i + b\color{#CD0000}ic -bd\color{#CD0000}{i^2}\) (\(\color{#CD0000}{i^2}\) is \(\color{#00CD00}{-1}\))
\(= (ac+bd) - (ad-bc)\color{#CD0000}i\)
